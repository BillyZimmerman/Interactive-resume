Technical Skills

  • Expert in Shopify theme development using Liquid templating, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to create responsive, conversion-optimized storefronts that drive sales and enhance user experience.
  • Proficient in creating custom Shopify sections and blocks that bring clients' unique visions to life, including custom hero banners, testimonial carousels, and branded content layouts that enhance storytelling and drive conversions.
  • Comprehensive understanding of metafields and template customization to create unique, tailored shopping experiences that align with brand identity and customer expectations.
  • Extensive experience working with diverse Shopify themes including Dawn, Debut, Horizon, Impulse, and custom-built themes, with the expertise to efficiently navigate, modify, and update any theme structure to meet evolving business needs and design requirements.
  • Committed to ADA compliance and web accessibility standards, implementing WCAG 2.1 guidelines to ensure inclusive shopping experiences for all users, including proper alt text, keyboard navigation, and screen reader compatibility.
  • Experience with adding Pixels and Google Analytics to track user behavior, conversions, and marketing effectiveness, enabling data-driven decision making.
